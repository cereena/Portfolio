About Me

Hello! I'm Cereena Binu, a passionate and motivated tech enthusiast currently diving deep into the world of full-stack web development. I'm pursuing MEARN stack training at Luminar Technolab, where I'm learning how to bring creative and functional websites to life using MongoDB, Express.js, Angular/React, and Node.js.

My journey began with a Bachelor's in Computer Applications, which gave me a strong foundation in programming and problem-solving. Since then, I've been eager to grow not just technically, but also personally. I love building visually appealing and user-friendly interfaces with HTML, CSS, Bootstrap, and Tailwind, and I'm slowly mastering JavaScript to make them interactive.

Apart from my technical path, I've also worked professionally as a Documentation Executive and Educational Counsellor for 9 months, which helped shape my communication, organizational, and client-handling skills. My goal is to become a confident and capable full-stack developer who can build solutions that make a real difference.

Whether it's solving a coding challenge, sketching out a new layout on Figma, or learning something new every day — I'm here for the journey, and I'm excited about what lies ahead.
